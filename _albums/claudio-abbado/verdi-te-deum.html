Verdi's Te Deum is well-served on YouTube with a recording with Claudio Abbado conducting the Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra, and the Wiener Konzertvereinigung and soprano soloist Cheryl Studer. Note, however, that this video is of all four of the Sacred Pieces, and the Te Deum starts at 25:26.
