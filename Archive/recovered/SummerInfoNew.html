Stanford Summer Chorus Information, 1999

NB: The information on this page is the best I have at this time, but is provisional and definitely subject to change.

Program

Requiem by Fauré

Performed by the Stanford Summer Chorus, Amy Stuart Hunn, Director

There will be a single performance Thursday, August 5th, at 8:00 PM in Memorial Church. Prices are $4 for students, $8 General Admission.

To Join

It's not hard.... just come to the first rehearsal! It's on Wednesday, June 23th.

Administrative Details

There is a $25.00 participation fee for anyone who is not a Stanford student. All music is provided free of charge.

Schedule

Rehearsals are Wednesdays, 7:00 to 9:30 PM, starting June 23th in Braun Rehearsal Hall.

The concert is on Thursday, August 5th at 8:00 PM. Call will be at 6:30.
