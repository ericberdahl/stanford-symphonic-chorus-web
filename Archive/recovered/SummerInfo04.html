Stanford Summer Chorus, 2004

Instructions for downloading sound files by parts of all our music can be found at http://www.stanford.edu/group/SymCh/SummerChorus/ReadMe.html
Thank you, Amy!

Amy Stuart Hunn will direct the Summer Chorus this year.

The program includes

  • Magnificat, Giovanni Battista Pergolesi
  • Cantata BWV 112, Der Herr ist mein getreuer Hirt, Johann Sebastian Bach
  • Heilig Felix Mendelssohn
  • Alleluia Randall Thompson
  • Hark, I Hear the Harps Eternal Alice Parker, arr.
  • Song for Athene (Alleluia) John Tavener
  • Elijah Rock Moses Hogan

The first two works are performed with string orchestra and soloists; the rest are a cappella.

To Join

It's not hard.... just come to the first rehearsal! Membership is open to everyone; singers typically come from all corners of the Bay Area, and from all age groups and ability levels. It's not a bad idea to come a little early to the first rehearsal to allow time to sign up.

Schedule

Rehearsals are Wednesdays, 7:15 to 9:45 PM, starting June 23th, in Campbell Recital Hall in Braun Music Center on the Stanford campus. (Directions) The performance will be Saturday, July 31, 2004, in Stanford Memorial Church (Mem Chu).

Fees

Participation is free for students; others will be charged $30. Music will be available for purchase at the first two rehearsals.

Questions?

Email to amy.hunn@stanfordalumni.org.
