CD1 has the files for soprano (tracks 1-10) and alto (11-20); CD2 has tenor (tracks 1-10) and bass (11-20).

Individual files may be found in the soprano, alto, tenor and bass directories, renamed to reflect their contents. Since I wasn't absolutely certain the files would still be readable after renaming, I left them with their original names in the directories CD1 and CD2 (or you can just download the tarred, gzipped version).

The contents of the tracks as they appear on CD1 and CD2 are

  1. Magnificat I
  2. Magnificat II
  3. Bach I
  4. Bach V
  5. Heilig, chorus 1
  6. Heilig, chorus 2
  7. Alleluia
  8. Hark
  9. Song of Athene
  10. Elijah
