Stanford Summer Chorus Information, 2001

Amy Stuart Hunn will be music director. The program includes

  • Te Deum, Kodaly
  • African American spirituals
  • Serenade to Music, Vaughn Williams
  • Fanfare for the Common Man, Copland

To Join

It's not hard.... just come to the first rehearsal! It's on Tuesday, June 26th at 7:15. It's not a bad idea to come a little early to the first rehearsal to allow time to sign up.

Schedule

Rehearsals are Tuesdays, 7:15 to 9:45 PM, starting June 26th, some in Campbell Recital Hall (CRH) within Braun Music Center, others at Dinkelspiel (Dink). Please read the detailed schedule below carefully! The performance will be Thursday, August 2, 2001, in Stanford Memorial Church (Mem Chu)

Fees

Participation is free for students; others will be charged $25. Music will be supplied for everyone.
