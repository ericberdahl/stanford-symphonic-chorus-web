Stanford Summer Chorus, 2003

Dr. Karen Kennedy will direct the Summer Chorus this year. She is Director of Choral Activities at the University of Hawai'i at Manoa and director of the Honolulu Symphony Chorus.

The concert will be all coronation music:

  • Coronation Anthems, Handel
  • Choral Dances from Gloriana, Britten
  • Crown Him with Many Crowns, Powell

To Join

It's not hard.... just come to the first rehearsal! It's on Wednesday, June 25th at 7:15 in Campbell Recital Hall. It's not a bad idea to come a little early to the first rehearsal to allow time to sign up.

Schedule

Rehearsals are Wednesdays, 7:15 to 9:45 PM, starting June 25th, in Campbell Recital Hall (CRH). The performance will be Saturday, August 2, 2003

Fees

Participation is free for students; others will be charged $30. Music will be available for purchase at the first rehearsal.
