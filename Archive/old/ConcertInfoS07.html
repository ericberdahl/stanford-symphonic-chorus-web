Stanford Symphonic Chorus Concert Information

Performances are most often in Memorial Church on the Stanford Campus (see map ).

Prices are usually $5 for students, $10 General Admission. Tickets are available at the door and from Stanford Symphonic Chorus members two weeks prior to the concert.
