Stanford Summer Chorus Information, 2000

Amy Stuart Hunn will be music director. The program will include Vesperae Solennes de Confessore, K. 339, by Mozart, Cantique de Jean Racine, Op. 11, by Gabriel Fauré, and Pange Lingua by Zoltán Kodály

To Join

It's not hard.... just come to the first rehearsal! It's on Wednesday, June 21st at 7:15 in Campbell Recital Hall.

Schedule

Rehearsals are Wednesdays, 7:15 to 9:45 PM, starting June 21st, in Campbell Recital Hall within Braun Music Center. The performance will be Thursday, August 3, 2000, in Stanford Memorial Church

NB: Rehearsal on July 26 will most likely be held on the Dink stage, as was the case for July 19.

Fees

Participation is free for students; others will be charged $25. Music will be supplied for everyone.
