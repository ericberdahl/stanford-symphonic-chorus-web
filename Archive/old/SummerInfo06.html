Stanford Summer Chorus, 2006

The Stanford Summer Chorus, a non-auditioned ensemble that rehearses and performs at Stanford University during summer term.

This summer the Chorus will be directed by Dr. Jim Dawson (wjdawson@stanford.edu), former University Organist at Stanford, and Professor of Music at St. Paul's University (Rikkyo University) in Tokyo.

For additional information, contact Dr. Dawson at wjdawson@stanford.edu). See also this poster, which includes information of interest to potential members of the chorus and audience.

Repertoire

Haydn: Lord Nelson Mass (Missa in Angustiis), with chorus, orchestra and soloists

Rehearsals/Concert Information

Rehearsals are Tuesday evenings from 7:15-9:45 PM, beginning on 27 June 2006 in Cambell Recital Hall, Braun Music Center, Stanford University. (Note that for the second week, the rehearsal is moved from the 4th of July, to Thursday, 6 July, same time.)

There is a $30 participation fee per person (waived for matriculated Stanford students and Stanford faculty), plus members purchase their own music, available at rehearsal.

The single performance will take place Saturday, 5 August 2006, 8 p.m. in Stanford Memorial Church
