Stanford Symphonic Chorus Concert Information

Performances are most often in Memorial Church on the Stanford Campus (see map ).

Prices are usually $4 for students, $8 General Admission. Tickets are available at the door and from the Stanford Box Office in Tresidder Union, (650) 725-ARTS.
