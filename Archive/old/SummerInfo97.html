Where

 The performance will be in Memorial Church on the Stanford Campus (see map ).

When

 Thursday, August 7th at 8:00 PM

For Members

To Join

It's not hard.... just come to the first rehearsal! It's on June 18th.

Administrative Details

There is a $25.00 participation fee for anyone who is not a Stanford student.

All music is provided free of charge.

Schedule

Rehearsals are Wednesdays, 7:00 to 9:30 PM, starting June 18th in Braun Rehearsal Hall. There is no rehearsal on July 2nd.

There is a single "dress rehearsal" (you are not expected to wear concert dress) on Wednesday, August 6th in the Church at the usual rehearsal time (7:00). The concert is on Thursday, August 7th at 8:00 PM. Call will be at 6:30.
