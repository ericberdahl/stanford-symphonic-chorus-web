How to Update the SSC Website - Performances

Performances

The introduction does not need to be updated.

Years

Each Winter quarter, add the new year at the top of the list below, and for the Spring and Fall quarters, change the href to link to the topmost quarter for the year.

  • YYYY
  • 2014
  • 2013
  • 2012
  • In the actual Performances page, the years go back to 1995. Some members have suggested going through private collections of old posters to reconstruct this record even further back.

Symphonic Chorus Home » Performances


The section for each quarter begins with a break tag, <br class="clear" />, followed by a div, <div id="qYYYY" class="middle">, where the id of the div is our code for the quarter and year. This div contains three parts:

  1. An image that appears on the left. When the poster or other suitable image is available, we code a link to mediabox and frame the thumbnail using a CSS class: 
    <a class="photo pgraph" href="performances/S2014/tinternabbey.jpg"
   rel="mediabox[590 590]"
   title="The Chancel and Crossing of Tintern Abbey, Looking towards the East Window, 1794 by J.M.W.Turner, pencil and watercolor on paper">
   <img class="framed"
        src="performances/S2014/tinternabbey.jpg" width="107" height="165"
        alt="The Chancel and Crossing of Tintern Abbey, Looking towards the East Window,
             1794 by J.M.W.Turner, pencil and watercolor on paper" />
</a>

    For how to construct the thumbnail image, see Expanding thumbnail images.

    Otherwise, we use this code to reference the Music at Stanford logo: 

    <img src="images/M@S-roundedges.gif" alt="" />

  2. A div containing details of the repertoire and performers for the center section: 
    <div class="middle_content">
   <h3>Quarter YYYY</h3>
   <p>Stanford Symphonic Chorus and any other group or groups by name:
      Title of the concert</p>
   <ul class="artists">
       <li>Composer 1, <em>Title of piece 1</em></li>
       <li>Composer 2, <em>Title of piece 2</em></li>
   </ul>
   <p>Directed by Director and performed in collaboration with
      the Another Named Group, 
      with Singer Name 1, baritone, etc.</p>
   <h4>Concerts:</h4>
   <p>Friday dd Month YYYY, 7:30 p.m., Location</p>
</div>
  3. A list of links that appears on the right. Not all of these list items need appear. The ticket office and map links should appear for a performance only if it has not happened yet: 
    <ul class="middle_links">
    <li><a href="http://sto.stanfordtickets.org/single/SelectSeating.aspx?p=5690">Tickets
        for Friday dd Mon 7:30 p.m. at Location of Concert</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://campus-map.stanford.edu/?id=&lat=37.4268766914&lng=-122.17046525&zoom=16&srch=Memorial Church"
        target="Memorial Church">Map to find Memorial Church</a></li>
    <li><a href="../performances/W2014/SSO-SSCVerdiPoster.pdf">Poster in PDF</a></li>
    <li><a href="../performances/W2014/SSO-SSCVerdiPoster.jpg">Poster in jpeg</a></li>
    <li><a href="../performances/W2010/FYLP.html">For Your Listening Pleasure</a></li> 
</ul>
